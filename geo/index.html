What can you see with open data?

Select a story from the options below. These visualizations utilize data published on the Edo State Open Data Portal.

Maps were made using Tilemill and hosted by Mapbox. You can use both of these tools for free.

Getting to school
Bus stops and their proximity to selected schools in Benin City.

Connecting at school
Electricity sources (such as transformers) near selected schools in Benin City.

Churches and shops
Over 70 religious institutions mapped alongside shops and bars in Benin City.

Healthy travel
Selected hotels and health care organizations mapped in Benin City.

Tracking illegal waste dumping
Legal and illegal waste dump locations in Benin City.

This website was made in support of the launch of the Edo State Open Data Portal. The goal of this website is to demonstrate how free and open tools can leverage open data for visual storytelling.

See each map legend for a link to the data source. In most cases, the data displayed on the map is a compilation of various datasets merged and cleaned up by the author; this master spreadsheet can be viewed here.

Tip: Use your mouse's scroll wheel to zoom.
